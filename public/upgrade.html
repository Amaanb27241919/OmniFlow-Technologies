Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my existing account?

Email us at akhan.industries@gmail.com with your ARIA account email and desired tier. We'll upgrade your access within 24 hours during the beta period.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — beta users receive 14 days of Pro access at no charge. Contact us to activate your trial before the public launch.

What is a "mission"?

A mission is a single ARIA research and advisory task — for example, "Analyze Tesla's supply chain resilience" or "Source mid-market buyout targets in industrials." Each mission runs the full agent pipeline and produces a structured PDF report.

What is WatchDog?

WatchDog monitors companies, competitors, or topics 24/7 using live search. It surfaces critical signals — earnings, leadership changes, regulatory actions — and sends you an email alert or Slack notification automatically.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes. During the beta period there are no long-term contracts. Email us to cancel or change your plan at any time.

Is my data private?

Yes. Each account's mission history, client data, and memory store are fully isolated. We do not use your mission data to train models or share it with third parties.
